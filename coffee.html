Coffee Butter Latte

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cookie butter
  • 1/2 cup hot coffee
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup hot milk

Steps

  1. Add cookie butter to hot coffee and whisk until cookie butter has dissolved. Stir in brown sugar and vanilla. Pour into a coffee mug.

  2. Froth hot milk with an immersion blender or with a frothing wand. Using the back of a spoon, pour frothed milk over coffee. Pour foam over frothed milk.
